Stored Information

This website stores personal information in order to make login easier. By storing details about the user, they can easily make another appointment at any time with their details already filled, saving time. If at any time you wish to withdraw access from the personal details stored, simply select decline and all of the previously stored details will be removed. After, if you would like the details to be stored again, click accept when opening the page.

By clicking 'Agree', you agree to your personal information being stored to faciliate the process of making a doctor's appointment.

By clicking 'Decline', you do not allow the form to store your personal details.
